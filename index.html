Dr. Ibtihaj Ahmad

Dr. Ibtihaj Ahmad is a Postdoctoral Fellow at the National Health and Medical Big Data Research Institute,Shandong University, China. His research focuses on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, with particular emphasis on medical image analysis, multimodal medical data, and intelligent healthcare systems. He has taught courses in Artificial Intelligence, Image Processing, and programming languages including Python, C++, and MATLAB in universities and open setups. He has also contributed to a number of research projects, including projects related to national-level funding such as the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) and National R&D funding (Pakistan).
